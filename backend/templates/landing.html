About Our System

Our IoT-Based Smart Diabetes Monitoring & Prediction System integrates wearable sensors and machine learning techniques to continuously monitor vital health parameters such as glucose levels, heart rate, and physical activity. The collected sensor data is transmitted in real time to a cloud-based backend system, where it is processed to assess the user’s diabetes risk.

The system provides a live and interactive dashboard for data visualization, risk analysis, and early health alerts. This enables users and healthcare professionals to make proactive and informed decisions, supporting effective and long-term diabetes management.
